What We Do

STRUCTURAL STEEL DETAILING

Structural steel detailing is a crucial aspect of the design and construction process in the field of civil engineering and architecture. It involves creating detailed drawings and plans that provide the information necessary for the fabrication and erection of structural steel components within a building or structure.

ELECTRICAL LAYOUT DRAWINGS

Electrical layout drawings are typically accompanied by schematic diagrams that provide a visual representation of the electrical circuits, connections, and equipment within the building

ADVANCED MODELING AND TAKEOFF

Advanced modelling for quantifying steel materials based on 3D models or 2D drawings. This includes counting steel members, measuring lengths, areas, and volumes, and calculating weights. This helps project managers and estimators accurately budget and plan for steel construction projects.

MISCELLANEOUS STEEL DETAILING

Miscellaneous steel detailing involves the detailed design and documentation of steel components that are not considered part of the primary structural system of a building or structure. These components often serve functional or decorative purposes and may include items such as stairs, handrails, ladders, platforms, catwalks, mezzanines, and miscellaneous supports
